Salt soup

Description

An old Polish dish popularized by the animated series Kapitan Bomba and its spin-off, Laser Gniew Dzida, which is a favorite delicacy of various types of illegal immigrants there. It is the easiest and cheapest soup in the world to prepare, beating even such culinary hits as broth, wodzionka, military pea soup or Chinese soup.

Ingredients

  • Any amount of water.
  • Any amount of salt.
  • Pot / old can / shoe / guitar case

Steps

  1. Component number 1 should be provided in component number 3
  2. After subjecting the mixture to heat treatment, sprinkle ingredient number 2
