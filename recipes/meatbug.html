Meatbug Ragout by Snaf

Description

Restores 20% of life energy. You can sell it for 3 gold, but buy it up to 10 gold.

Ingredients

  • 3 meatbugs
  • 5 Hell mushrooms
  • 2 bowels of rice

Steps

  1. Throw chopped beetles into water and wait till the boiling starts
  2. Throw chopped Hell mushrooms and add rice
  3. Mix everything up
